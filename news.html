Stratford

Description

Stratford, a small town nestled in the Golden Horshoe in Ontario. A town named for Shakespeares birthplace has a lot to offer. It's farmers market is one of the best in the area and it also includes some spectacular plays that can be showcased in theatres throughout the city

What to do

You could enjoy a nice coffee at the famous Balzac's Coffee Shop along with a nice stroll along the Avon River or enjoy a meal in the market. There are also many plays that play during the Stratford Festival that captivate the eye and the ear.

How to get there

You could take the train from Toronto Union Station or drive along the 401 and then turning off the exit for the Ontario Route 8.

Orlando

Description

Orlando, the home of Disney World and Universal Studios is a world renowned city known for its vast amusement parks and many themed hotels

What to do

Visit the many themed parks strategically scattered throughout the Orlando area or go into town and experience some hard-core southern cuisine. Tampa and Coco Beach are both around 1:30mins away from Orlando and you can visit the Kennedy Space Center in Coco Beach

How to get there

Orlando International Airport offers flights from the U.S, Canada, Central and South America and Europe. Amtrak offers services from New York, Miami, Los Angeles and many other locations. Orlando can also be reahed by car using Interstate 4.
